# Murphy's Law Travel Examples

Travel turns timing into a stress test. Flights, buses, luggage, weather, and documents all create useful Murphy's Law examples.

## Airport example

The short connection is delayed just enough to miss the next flight, which leaves on time for the first time all week.

## Luggage example

Your bag appears last unless the connection is tight, in which case it appears somewhere else.

## Reduce the risk

- Leave buffer time where recovery is hardest.
- Keep essentials in a carry-on.
- Save offline copies of tickets and confirmations.
