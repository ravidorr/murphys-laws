# Murphy's Law Work Examples

Work is full of small coordination failures that become large at exactly the wrong time.

## Meeting example

The one stakeholder needed for a decision is the one person who cannot attend. Related laws live in [Murphy's Office Laws](/category/murphys-office-laws).

## Deadline example

The task marked "quick fix" becomes a dependency investigation after the deadline is announced.

## Reduce the risk

- Confirm owners before the meeting.
- Write the fallback decision path.
- Test the handoff before the final hour.
