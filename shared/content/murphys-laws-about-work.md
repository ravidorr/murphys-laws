# Murphy's Laws About Work

Work is where Murphy's Law gets a calendar invite. Deadlines, meetings, handoffs, status reports, and unclear ownership all create perfect conditions for predictable failure.

## Common work patterns

- The decision you need is waiting on the person who is out today.
- The email error becomes visible only after sending.
- The meeting that should solve confusion creates three follow-up meetings.

## How to use these laws

Use work-related Murphy's Laws as planning prompts. Ask what can be misunderstood, what can wait until too late, and what backup plan needs to exist before the deadline.
