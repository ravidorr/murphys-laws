# Everyday Murphy's Law Examples

Everyday Murphy's Law examples are the small failures that feel personally timed.

## Toast example

Buttered toast waits for the cleanest floor before testing gravity. Try the [Buttered Toast Calculator](/calculator/buttered-toast) to model the joke.

## Errand example

The line you choose becomes the slowest line, and your original line speeds up after you leave it.

## Reduce the risk

- Add time buffers for errands with dependencies.
- Keep spare chargers, copies, and basic backups.
- Assume the small missing item is the one you will need.
